# Resume Templates

## Recommended Templates

These are recommended platforms for creating your resume:

- [FlowCV](https://flowcv.com) — Very customization-friendly, free, and easy to use. (Recommended)
- [Canva](https://canva.com)
- [Resume IO](https://resume.io)
- [Novo Resume](https://novoresume.com)

Pick the one that fits you best. The key is to use a standard, professional template rather than building from scratch — this ensures proper formatting and ATS compatibility.
